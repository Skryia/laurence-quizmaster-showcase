Meet Laurence

The host who turns a room of strangers into a roaring quiz team.

For more than fifteen years, Laurence has been the warm, witty voice behind hundreds of quiz nights across the UK — from cosy village pubs to glittering hotel ballrooms.

Every quiz is written from scratch and tailored to the venue, the audience and the occasion. Expect smart questions, plenty of laughs and a tightly run night that finishes on a high.
